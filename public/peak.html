How do I get my Basis Peak data?

Export your Basis Peak Data to .csv by selecting export data in app settings. It will be best if you decide to export ALL your data. (How to export my Basis Peak Data?)

Why are you asking some personal data?

All your data are anonymes. However we need to know a little about yourself in order to have data that can be used in scientific study.
